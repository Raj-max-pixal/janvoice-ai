# JanVoice AI RC1 Release Notes

## Overview
JanVoice AI is a civic engagement platform that helps citizens submit feedback, authorities triage urgent needs, and communities monitor progress through a transparent digital experience.

## Highlights
- Modern landing experience with clearer product messaging
- Citizen dashboard for complaints, voice notes, and evidence uploads
- MP and admin views for triage, tracking, and oversight
- Public feed and analytics surfaces for transparency
- Responsive UI with dark mode and polished visual design

## Production Readiness Notes
- The project now builds successfully with Vite and TypeScript.
- Demo-friendly behavior is enabled when Firebase credentials are not configured.
- The app is suitable for local demos, staging review, and further deployment hardening.

## Next Steps
- Connect real Firebase credentials and Firestore rules.
- Add production-grade authentication and storage handling.
- Expand analytics and AI workflows with live data pipelines.
